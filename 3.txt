Aap apna Aadhaar card online download karne ke liye UIDAI ke official website ka use kar sakte ho. Niche step-by-step process diya gaya hai:

---

### **Steps to Download Aadhaar Card Online**

#### **1. Visit the UIDAI Website**
- Official website par jaayein: [https://uidai.gov.in](https://uidai.gov.in)

---

#### **2. Go to the Aadhaar Download Section**
- "My Aadhaar" section me click karein.
- Wahan aapko **Download Aadhaar** option milega.

---

#### **3. Select the Download Option**
- Aapko teen options milenge:
  - **Aadhaar Number (UID)**: Agar aapke paas Aadhaar number hai.
  - **Enrollment ID (EID)**: Agar Aadhaar banate waqt aapko enrollment slip mili hai.
  - **Virtual ID (VID)**: Agar aapke paas VID hai.

---

#### **4. Enter Details**
- Jo bhi option aapne select kiya hai, uske corresponding details bharni hongi:
  - Aadhaar/Enrollment/Virtual ID number.
  - CAPTCHA code.

---

#### **5. OTP Verification**
- Registered mobile number par ek OTP bheja jayega.
- OTP enter karne ke baad verification process complete karein.

---

#### **6. Download e-Aadhaar**
- Successfully verify hone ke baad, aapka **e-Aadhaar** PDF format me download ho jayega.

---

### **How to Open the Aadhaar PDF?**
- Downloaded file **password-protected** hoti hai.
- Password:
  - Aapka **First 4 letters of your name in CAPITAL letters** + **Birth Year (YYYY)**.

  **Example**:  
  - Name: Rahul Sharma  
  - Birth Year: 1995  
  - Password: **RAHU1995**

---

### **Key Points:**
1. Aapka registered mobile number Aadhaar ke saath hona chahiye OTP ke liye.
2. e-Aadhaar downloaded version digitally signed hota hai, aur ye valid proof hai.

Agar mobile number linked nahi hai, toh pehle apna mobile number update karwana padega Aadhaar Seva Kendra par jaake.
